Android App: DysScreen

DysScreen is an app that being designed to address the challenge of early detection of dyslexia in children. The objective of the app is to provide a reliable tool for parents, educators, and guardians to screen and assess children suspected of having dyslexia. DysScreen app that facilitates user-friendly assessments and generates personalized results, aiding in the early identification of dyslexia.

The core functionalities of DysScreen App, including language selection of the app, assessment and screening tests, test result generation, and access to dyslexia-related information. The emphasis is on creating a basic, visual representation to convey the flow and interaction within the app.

Tools used: Figma, Flutter, Dart

Android App: BarterIt

BarterIt is a mobile app that allows users to barter unused items with each other. It's a great way to get rid of unwanted items, find new things to use, and save money. To use BarterIt, simply create an account and start browsing the items that are available. You can search for specific items, or browse by category. Once you find an item that you're interested in, you can contact the seller to make a trade. BarterIt is a great way to declutter your home, save money, and help the environment. By trading used items, you can keep them out of landfills and give them a new life with someone else.

The core functionalities of Barterit App, including account creation, item listing, item searching.

Tools used: Figma, Flutter, Dart, PHP, MySQL
